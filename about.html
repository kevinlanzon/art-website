About

Following on from completing a foundation course at the Kent Institute of Art and Design Janie continued her studies at the University of Brighton, from where she graduated with a BA Hons in Three Dimensional Craft. After completing a PGCE in Secondary Education Janie went on to teach Design and Technology and Fine Art Graphics.Â Janie now focusses on her own painting and delivers occasional workshops.

Janie takes her inspiration from the everyday, the beauty in the objects that enable us to go about our activities without giving them a second thought. Her work adopts a wide range of media, often re-employing discarded paintings and paraphernalia to facilitate the recording of everyday scenes and objects in her loose and rapid style. A recent body of paintings has explored natural groupings of objects, cutlery in a drawer, books on a shelf, in contrast to the traditional contrived still life composition.
